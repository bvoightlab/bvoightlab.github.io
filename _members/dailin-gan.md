Dailin joined the Voight Lab as a postdoctoral researcher in June 2026.

He received his Ph.D. in Statistics from the University of Notre Dame, where he developed interpretable statistical and machine learning methods for single-cell and high-dimensional genomics data. Prior to that, he received his Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the University of Hong Kong.

His current research focuses on statistical genetics and human disease, with a broad interest in linking genetic variation to disease mechanisms through principled statistical and computational approaches.
